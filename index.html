About us

We are a locally owned family restaurant offering a mix of authentic Chinese and Chinese-Canadian cuisine. After 18 years of serving another community, we are so excited to share our passion for delicious food with the beautiful city of St. John's!

"Buffet?!" Yes, we are serving a buffet! To give our customers the choice to eat within their own dietary restrictions, and at their own pace, is something we value greatly. Our selection is smaller but carefully curated to ensure freshness and quality for our customers. Our goal is to ensure that all of our dishes are enjoyed by our clientele.

Not a buffet person? No problem! We're also offering a variety of menus for both dine-in and takeout! Our menu also features a number of both vegetarian- and gluten-friendly items, but make sure to inform our server of any severe dietary restrictions or allergies!

We always believe that GOOD FOOD and GOOD SEVICE make
HAPPY CUSTOMERS!
